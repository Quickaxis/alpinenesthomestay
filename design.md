# Alpine Nest Homestay Website Design Specifications
### Style: Apple × Airbnb Luxe × Glassmorphism × Flyer Composition
### Main Theme: Ivory, Deep Forest Black, and Neon Green

---

## Brand Colors

- **Primary Background**: `#F8F7F2` (Ivory) & `#FFFFFF` (Pure White)
- **Dark Section Background**: `#0E1512` & `#111A15` (Deep Forest Black)
- **Primary Accent**: `#A8FF1E` (Neon Green / Lime)
- **Secondary Green**: `#5D7A55` (Forest Green)
- **Soft Sage**: `#DDE8D7`
- **Text**: `#101010` (Charcoal Black) & `#4F4F4F` (Slate Gray)
- **Glass Panels**: `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.45)`
- **Glass Blur**: `backdrop-filter: blur(28px)`
- **Glass Border**: `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.35)`

---

## Typography

- **Heading Font**: Manrope / Satoshi / General Sans (ExtraBold)
- **Body Font**: Inter / Manrope
- **Typography Scale**:
  - Hero Heading: `80px` (Desktop) | `42px` (Mobile)
  - Hero Styling: Very bold (`font-weight: 900`), tight spacing (`-2px` letter-spacing)
  - Section Heading: `56px`
  - Sub Heading: `26px`
  - Body: `18px`
  - Small Text: `15px`
  - Button Text: `17px`

---

## Design System

- **Border Radii**:
  - Cards: `36px`
  - Images: `32px`
  - Buttons: `999px`
  - Navbar: `999px`
  - Inputs: `22px`
  - Glass Panels: `36px`
- **Shadows**:
  - Soft default shadow: `0 20px 65px rgba(14, 21, 18, 0.05)`
  - Glass shadow: `0 15px 40px rgba(14, 21, 18, 0.03)`
  - Hover shadow: `0 30px 80px rgba(14, 21, 18, 0.08)`
  - Neon glow shadow: `0 10px 40px rgba(168, 255, 30, 0.12)`
- **Layout Vibe**:
  - Floating glass pill navbar.
  - Overlapping room cards behind a main glass folder card.
  - Generous white spaces, dark and ivory sections contrast, and soft blurred backgrounds.

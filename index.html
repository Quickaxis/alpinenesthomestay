Stay Guidelines

House Rules & Policies

To ensure a comfortable, hygienic, and secure environment for all our guests, we request you to review and observe the following guidelines during your stay.

01 No Smoking Inside

Smoking is strictly prohibited inside any of the guest rooms or indoor suites to maintain freshness and safety. Dedicated outdoor spots are available.

02 Quiet Hours After 10:00 PM

To respect other residents and fellow travelers, playing loud music or hosting noisy gatherings is strictly restricted after 10:00 PM.

03 Maintain Cleanliness

We pride ourselves on hygiene. Please keep the rooms, common spaces, and private kitchens tidy. Daily room cleaning is available.

04 Respect Other Guests

Treat shared property utilities and staff members with courtesy. High-quality hospitality relies on mutual respect.

05 Government ID Required

All domestic and international travelers must present a valid government-issued photo ID card (Aadhaar, Passport, Driving License) at check-in.

06 Damage Charges Applicable

Any breakage or damage caused to the furniture, appliances, or properties during your stay will be charged directly to the checking account.
